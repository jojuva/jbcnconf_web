Networking Party

Barcelona Beach - Barceloneta

How about meeting other JVM enthusiasts facing a beautiful beach in Barcelona?
Come to our sponsors' booth and ask for your ticket for our networking party!
Hurry up! Tickets are limited to 200, only those with the ticket will be allowed to enter in the party venue.

Dinner starts at 20.00.

We will have a bus that will take us from the conference venue to the party place, on Monday at 19.00, please don’t be late.

Location: Passeig del Mare Nostrum, 19-21, 08039 Barcelona
Venue: Mamarosa Beach Restaurant
